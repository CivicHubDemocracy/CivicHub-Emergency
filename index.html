🚨 UNIDENTIFIED AGENT SAFETY PROTOCOLS

⚠️ IF APPROACHED BY UNIDENTIFIED AGENTS

  1. DEMAND IDENTIFICATION FIRST
  2. Say: "Show me your badge and identification"
  3. Say: "State your agency and purpose"
  4. Say: "I do not consent to any search or detention"
  5. DO NOT RESIST PHYSICALLY - Document everything
  6. Say loudly: "I am being detained by unidentified persons"
  7. Immediately call 911 and emergency hotlines

📋 AGENT VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

  • Valid federal badge with photo ID
  • Agency identification (ICE, CBP, etc.)
  • Supervisor contact information
  • Warrant or legal authority documents
  • Reason for contact/detention
  • Agents MUST identify themselves when asked

📱 EMERGENCY DOCUMENTATION

  • Start recording immediately (if safe)
  • Note badge numbers, vehicle plates, faces
  • Record all verbal exchanges
  • Share live location with trusted contacts
  • Livestream to social media if possible
  • Call witnesses to observe and record

🛡️ COMMUNITY SAFETY NETWORKS

  • Establish buddy check-in systems
  • Create group chat rapid response networks
  • Share location with multiple trusted contacts
  • Set up regular check-in times daily
  • Plan emergency meeting locations
  • Practice de-escalation scenarios
  • Know legal observer contact numbers
